Our Data Types

Our platform hosts a wide range of high-quality datasets used to power machine learning and AI systems. From images to text and videos, each type of data plays a crucial role in building smarter technologies. Here's an overview of the most common data formats you'll find.

Learn more

Image Data

Visual information for AI vision systems

Image data includes photos, scanned documents, satellite imagery, medical images, and more. These datasets are used in applications like facial recognition, object detection, OCR, and autonomous navigation. Formats typically include JPG, PNG, and TIFF.

Text Data

Language data for NLP and content analysis

Text datasets consist of news articles, product reviews, customer feedback, code snippets, legal documents, and conversational transcripts. They're essential for training AI to understand and generate human language. Common formats are TXT, CSV, JSON, and DOCX.

Video Data

Dynamic data for motion and behavior analysis

Video data includes surveillance clips, instructional videos, interviews, and driving footage. These datasets support applications such as activity recognition, gesture detection, and video summarization. Common formats include MP4, MOV, and AVI.
